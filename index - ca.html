California Birth and Death

This map represents California birth and death by county. Data spans the period 2010 - 2019. Data for this period was obtained from United States Census Bureau and Simple Maps.

Objectives

The data covers incorporated areas in the forty-five of the fifty-eight California counties.

For the purpose of this exercise, the most populous cities for each county was used as the centroid.
